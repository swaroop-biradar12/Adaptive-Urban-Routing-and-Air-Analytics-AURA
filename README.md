# 🌍 Adaptive Urban Routing and Air Analytics (AURA)

## 📌 Overview

Adaptive Urban Routing and Air Analytics (AURA) is an intelligent urban mobility platform that combines real-time traffic analysis, air quality monitoring, and adaptive route optimization to provide safer, healthier, and more efficient travel routes.

The system helps users make informed transportation decisions by considering factors such as:

- 🚗 Traffic congestion
- 🌫️ Air pollution levels
- ⏱️ Travel time
- 📍 Route efficiency
- 🌱 Environmental impact

AURA aims to promote sustainable urban transportation and improve public health through data-driven route recommendations.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Traditional navigation systems primarily focus on minimizing travel time or distance. However, they often ignore environmental factors such as air pollution, which can significantly affect public health.

AURA addresses this challenge by integrating:

- Real-time traffic data
- Air quality analytics
- Smart route optimization

to recommend routes that balance efficiency and environmental safety.

---

## ✨ Features

### 🚦 Adaptive Route Optimization
- Calculates optimal routes based on traffic conditions.
- Dynamically adjusts routes when conditions change.

### 🌫️ Air Quality Monitoring
- Tracks pollution levels across urban areas.
- Identifies pollution hotspots.

### 📊 Data Analytics Dashboard
- Visualizes traffic and air quality data.
- Displays trends and insights.

### 🗺️ Interactive Mapping
- Real-time route visualization.
- Pollution-aware navigation.

### 📈 Environmental Impact Analysis
- Evaluates exposure to pollutants.
- Encourages eco-friendly travel decisions.
